# IFAM Example: Repurposed Solar Lights for Homes

## Overview

A simple but powerful community innovation:

Outdoor solar motion security lights can be repurposed for indoor use to provide lighting during power outages and for everyday household needs — without depending on the electrical grid.

This idea uses an existing affordable product in a new way to support families, especially in energy-scarce regions.

---

## Why This Matters

This innovation shows how:

• simple observations can solve real problems  
• communities can adapt available tools creatively  
• affordable solutions can scale quickly  
• people can become contributors, not just recipients  

It represents the spirit of idea-gifting and local ingenuity.
