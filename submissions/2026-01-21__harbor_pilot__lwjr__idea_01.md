## 4) The Idea (simple description)
**Description:**  
Create a Harbor where people can submit practical ideas and innovations without expensive patent systems.  
Each submission is recorded in plain language, time-anchored (timestamped/hashed), and preserved with the giver’s name attached.  
The Harbor is built to support underserved communities and developing nations by enabling safe outbound idea-gifting and inbound seeking.

## 5) Why it helps (who benefits?)
**Benefit:**  
Students, builders, and communities can access and improve ideas while preserving origin attribution and dignity.  
This helps ideas travel home and forward without being erased or extracted.
